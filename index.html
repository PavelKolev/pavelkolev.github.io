Pavel Kolev

*

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems (MPI-IS), working in the Autonomous Learning Group with Georg Martius.

Prior that I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF), working in the Algorithms and Complexity Department with Kurt Mehlhorn.

I obtained my doctoral degree on 2018 in the Computer Science Department at Saarland University, where I was fortunate to be advised by Kurt Mehlhorn and Karl Bringmann. During my doctoral studies, I was also affiliated with the Algorithms and Complexity Department at Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF) and the Cluster of Excellence on "Multimodal Computing and Interaction (MMCI)".


Email: pavel.kolevtuebingen.mpg.de

Links: LinkedIn | GitHub | DBLP | Google Scholar

Research Interests

    I am interested in Machine Learning and Optimization. In particular, in extending and combining novel algorithmic techniques from the areas of reinforcement learning, combinatorial and convex optimization, and dynamical systems, so as to design efficient algorithms with provable guarantees that approximately solve challenging real-world problems.
